Crystal Game

You will be given a random number a the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to random number, you lose the game in your total score goes above random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click it.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each crystal.
